StrataSphere Building Facilities

Swimming Pool

Our heated pool is available to all residents and their guests.

Location: Level 5, Building A

Capacity: 30 people

Opening Hours:

  • Monday - Friday: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Saturday - Sunday: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Rules:

  • Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult
  • No glass containers in the pool area
  • Shower before entering the pool
  • No diving

Gym

Our fully equipped gym is available to all residents.

Location: Level 5, Building A (next to pool)

Capacity: 15 people

Opening Hours:

  • 24/7 access with key fob

Equipment:

  • Treadmills (3)
  • Elliptical machines (2)
  • Exercise bikes (2)
  • Weight machines
  • Free weights
  • Yoga mats and exercise balls

Rules:

  • No guests without resident present
  • Clean equipment after use
  • Return weights to racks
  • No children under 16
