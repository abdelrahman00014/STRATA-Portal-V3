StrataSphere Pet Policy

Effective Date: February 15, 2025

Document Version: 1.3

1. General Pet Guidelines

The StrataSphere Building strives to be pet-friendly while ensuring the comfort and safety of all residents. This policy establishes guidelines for pet ownership within the building.

2. Permitted Pets

The following pets are permitted in the building:

  • Dogs (maximum of two per unit, with combined weight not exceeding 50 pounds)
  • Cats (maximum of two per unit)
  • Small caged birds
  • Fish in aquariums up to 20 gallons
  • Small caged rodents (hamsters, guinea pigs, etc.)

3. Registration and Fees

3.1. All pets must be registered with the building management office within 7 days of moving in or acquiring a new pet.

3.2. A one-time pet registration fee of $200 applies for dogs and cats.

3.3. Pet owners must provide proof of current vaccinations and licenses as required by local regulations.

4. Pet Owner Responsibilities

4.1. Pet owners are responsible for their pet's behavior and must ensure pets do not disturb other residents.

4.2. Dogs must be kept on leashes at all times in common areas.

4.3. Pet waste must be immediately cleaned up and properly disposed of.

4.4. Pets are not permitted in the pool area, fitness center, or other recreational facilities.

4.5. Pet owners are responsible for any damage caused by their pets to building property or common areas.

5. Prohibited Animals

The following animals are not permitted in the building:

  • Exotic animals (reptiles, arachnids, etc.)
  • Farm animals
  • Any animals kept for breeding or commercial purposes
  • Any animal exceeding the size/weight restrictions
