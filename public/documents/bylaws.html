StrataSphere Building Bylaws

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Document Version: 2.1

1. General Provisions

1.1. These bylaws govern the StrataSphere Building and are binding on all owners, residents, and visitors.

1.2. The strata committee is responsible for enforcing these bylaws and may impose fines for violations as outlined in Section 8.

1.3. Owners are responsible for ensuring that their tenants, visitors, and guests comply with these bylaws.

2. Noise and Nuisance

2.1. Residents shall not create noise that unreasonably disturbs other residents, particularly between the hours of 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM.

2.2. Construction and renovation work that creates noise is permitted only between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM on weekdays, and 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM on Saturdays. No such work is permitted on Sundays or public holidays.

2.3. Musical instruments, stereos, televisions, and other sound-producing devices must be used at reasonable volume levels.

3. Common Areas

3.1. Common areas must be kept clean and free of personal belongings.

3.2. No items may be stored in hallways, stairwells, or other common areas without prior approval from the strata committee.

3.3. Smoking is prohibited in all indoor common areas, including hallways, elevators, and stairwells.

4. Parking

4.1. Residents must park only in their designated parking spaces.

4.2. Visitor parking is limited to 24 hours unless prior approval is obtained from the building manager.

4.3. Vehicles that are inoperable, unregistered, or in disrepair may not be parked on the property.

5. Garbage and Recycling

5.1. All garbage must be properly bagged and placed in the designated dumpsters.

5.2. Recycling must be sorted according to the guidelines posted in the garbage room.

5.3. Large items must not be left in the garbage room and must be disposed of according to local regulations.
